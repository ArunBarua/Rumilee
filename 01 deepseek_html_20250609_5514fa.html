Honeymoon Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Beach Welcome

▼

Arrive at Port Blair airport where you'll be greeted with fresh flower garlands. Transfer to your luxury beachfront resort. Enjoy a romantic sunset walk on Radhanagar Beach, ranked among Asia's best beaches. End your day with a private candlelit dinner on the beach.

Day 2: Island Exploration

▼

After breakfast, embark on a private boat tour to the nearby islands. Snorkel together in the crystal clear waters of Jolly Buoy Island. Visit the limestone caves at Baratang Island. Enjoy a romantic picnic lunch on a secluded beach. Return to resort for spa treatments for two.

Day 3: Underwater Adventure

▼

Experience the underwater world together with a guided scuba diving session (no certification needed). After lunch, visit the historic Cellular Jail and witness the moving light and sound show. In the evening, enjoy a private sunset cruise with champagne and canapés.

Day 4: Relaxation & Departure

▼

Enjoy a leisurely breakfast in bed. Spend the morning relaxing at the resort's infinity pool or beach. Optional spa treatments before departure. Transfer to the airport with a special farewell gift, taking with you unforgettable memories of your romantic Andaman getaway.
