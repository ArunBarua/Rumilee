Our Story

Rumilee: Where Love Meets Light

Inspired by the 13th-century poet Rumi and love's eternal light, Rumilee crafts journeys where every moment is "made glorious by the light of love." We weave poetry into prose, transforming soft breezes on moonlit beaches into chapters of your own love story.

Our name "Rumilee" merges the timeless wisdom of Rumi with the concept of love as light ("lee"), illuminating each moment of your romantic escape. As Rumi wrote: "Both light and shadow are the dance of Love. Love has no cause; it is the astrolabe of God's secrets."

At Rumilee, we believe in crafting intimate experiences that linger in your heart long after the tan fades. From secluded beaches to candlelit dinners under the stars, we transform the breathtaking Andaman Islands into the canvas for your love story.

4 Nights / 5 Days Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Historic Charm

Arrive in Port Blair and unwind at a luxury beachfront resort. In the evening, visit the historic Cellular Jail and experience its famous Light & Sound Show under the stars, a poignant beginning to your journey.

Day 2: Island Paradise

Transfer to Havelock Island; spend the afternoon at Radhanagar Beach (voted one of Asia's best) enjoying sun and surf. At sunset, watch the sky blaze over the horizon and later indulge in a private candle-lit dinner on the sand.

Day 3: Underwater Wonders

Explore the underwater world together with a couples' scuba dive or snorkeling at Elephant Beach (the coral reef teems with life). Return on a glass-bottom boat for more marine views, then whisk off to tranquil Neil Island for a quiet evening stroll.

Day 4: Natural Wonders

Watch the sunrise at Sitapur Beach on Neil, then visit the famous natural rock bridge. In the afternoon return to Port Blair and relax at Carbyn Cove's lily-covered lagoon, capping the day with a beachside supper.

Day 5: Farewell with Memories

After a leisurely breakfast, bid farewell to Andaman and depart for home, carrying memories illuminated by love.
